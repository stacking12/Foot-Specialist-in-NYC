Understanding Plantar Fasciitis

Plantar fasciitis develops when the thick band of tissue along the bottom of your foot becomes inflamed. You may feel:

  • Sharp heel pain in the morning
  • Discomfort after long periods of standing
  • Increased pain after physical activity

Early treatment by a qualified Heel Specialist prevents worsening symptoms and helps you return to normal activities faster.
